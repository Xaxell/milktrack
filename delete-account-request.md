# Delete Account and Data Request

**Last updated:** [02-02-2026]

## Request Account Deletion
Users of the MilkTrack app may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

To request account deletion, please follow the steps below.

## How to Request Deletion
Send an email to the address below with the subject line:

**Subject:** Delete My Account

In the email, please include:
- Your registered mobile number or email address
- The reason for deletion (optional)

**Email:** [info@xaxell.com]

## What Data Will Be Deleted
Upon verification of the request, the following data will be permanently deleted:
- User account information
- Milk subscription details
- Delivery history
- Payment records
- Any disputes or adjustment records

## Data Retention
Some information may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as compliance or fraud prevention). Any retained data will not be used for active processing.
